**Master multiple technologies and concepts needed to ace your technical interviews!**

## What is Devcoder Knowledge Base?

This is a comprehensive, well-organized resource covering:

- **Java** - Core concepts, design patterns, multithreading, collections
- **JavaScript & TypeScript** - ES6+, async programming, type safety
- **React** - Hooks, state management, performance optimization

- **Interview Tips & Tricks** - Strategies and best practices
